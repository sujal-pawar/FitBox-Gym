Exercises

Squats: A lower body exercise where you bend your knees and hips to lower your body and then return to standing.

Plank: A core exercise where you hold a push-up position while keeping your body straight from head to heels.

Push-ups:An upper body exercise performed by raising and lowering the body using the arms while keeping the body straight.

Deadlift: A full-body lift where you pick up a weighted barbell from the ground to hip level while keeping your back straight.

Bench Press: A chest exercise where you lie on a bench and press a weighted barbell or dumbbells up and down.

Pull-ups:An upper body exercise performed by lifting your body up to a bar until your chin is above it.
